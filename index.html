About Us

Pragati is the annual technology fest of MCKV Institute of Engineering. Every year the college holds this annual fest which is organized by the college students. Truly an event for the engineers, by the engineers and with the engineers. This is the place where participants can self analyse themselves as well as show off their creative skills in a lot of fields. Participants from various Engineering colleges can compete with other participants in fields like Robotics, Tech quiz, Treasure hunts , Non-tech quiz, Robo wars, Coding and various other gaming events. All winners can get a chance to win attractive cash prizes.
